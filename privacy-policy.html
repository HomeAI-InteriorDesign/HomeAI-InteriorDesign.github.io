Home AI - Interior Design Arch Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [22.09.2024]

Home AI - Interior Design Arch application provides a service that allows users to take photos of their homes and rooms and redesign them using artificial intelligence. To protect your privacy, we have created this privacy policy. By using our application, you agree to the terms and practices outlined below regarding the collection and use of your personal information.

Information We Collect

While using the application, we only collect photos of the user's homes and rooms. These photos are used solely for the purpose of the AI redesign process.

Data Usage

The collected photos are used by the AI during the design process, and once the design is completed, these photos are deleted from our system. We do not store users' photos or share them with any third parties.

Photo Storage

The redesigned photos are saved directly to the users' devices. The application does not retain any of the users' photos in any form.

Privacy Protection

Protecting users' privacy is our priority. We take the necessary measures to process and safeguard user information securely within our application.

Third Parties

Our application does not share users' photos with third parties or transfer this information to any external sources.

Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child, we will promptly delete that information.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes through the application or by updating this page. Users will need to accept the updated policy for it to take effect.
